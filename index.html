Campus Life



School Classrooms

Our classrooms are bench mark of excellent infrastructure-- spacious and bright that support and encourage the smooth flow of academic interactions. The school has adopted a world-class comprehensive technology providing teaching solution with world-class teaching resources to aid teachers with animations, clips and pictures from a vast repository.



School Library

Our library functions in a multi dimensional pattern. It is a learners shelter, treasure house of information and enables them to venture into the world of books. It creates an environment of book -reviewing, story telling, picture manifestation in prose and even influences the pupils with poetic illusions. Of course all these happen under specialised- guidance of experts.



Transport Facilty

School provides a well integrated transport system made available to all categories of students. While providing connectivity and easy travel, it caters to both far and near ends in the district of Jammu city.
It is an eleborate administrative setup that has goals and objectives aligned with safety and time management.
